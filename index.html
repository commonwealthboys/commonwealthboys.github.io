ABOUT

The Commonwealth Boys are two dudes that do things, and sometimes those things cosmically align to make sounds that some individuals find aesthetically pleasing to the ear.

In short, we're just two dudes wandering space, making music and following the journey, wherever it takes us.

Formerly based out of San Fransisco, CA, and now based wherever this music finds its way to your ear drums, we hope you enjoy listening to our tunes as much as we did creating them.

Hope to be seeing you soon,

- The Boys
